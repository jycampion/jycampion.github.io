Biography

Medical student at the University of Tours. I work on neuroimaging, neuroinformatics and Machine and Deep Learning. I recently worked on neuroimaging as part of the Long COVID. My PhD in neuroscience will investigate a new way to train an AI classifier for better neurological and psychiatric diagnosis based on neuroimaging. We will first test a new preprocessing with a surface, multimodality and projection approach. Then, we will test GNN solutions with graph representations.
